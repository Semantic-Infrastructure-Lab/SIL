**His goal is to replace the wood with steel.**

1.  **The Loss of Truth:** Without provenance, we cannot know what is real.

He is building SIL not to create a product to sell, but to create a **Semantic Operating System**—a shared, open substrate that guarantees meaning, provenance, and invariants for the next century of computing.
